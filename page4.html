Concussions in the NFL and Their Effect on Team Performance

Data Mining

This project created a locally hosted app intended to explore the history of concussions in the NFL and how it affected teams' performance. It builds a network of NFL players, with attributes for team, position, concussions, years, weeks injured, games missed, play after, and play before, and edges between nodes connecting players who have played on the same team.
